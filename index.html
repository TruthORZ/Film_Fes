Everyone is a Universe

"We look to the stars, only to find ourselves."

About

For centuries, humanity has looked beyond the Earth, searching for signs of life in the vast unknown. But what if this search has never truly been about “them”?

From distant planets to incomprehensible signals, science fiction cinema repeatedly returns to a central question: what do we see when we encounter the alien?

Across five films, we invite you to look into the unknown—not to find answers, but to encounter reflections of memory, longing, belief, and the fragile limits of what it means to be human.
